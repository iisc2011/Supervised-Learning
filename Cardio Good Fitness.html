Data Dictionary

The data is about customers of the treadmill product(s) of a retail store called Cardio Good Fitness. It contains the following variables-

  1. Product - The model no. of the treadmill
  2. Age - Age of the customer in no of years
  3. Gender - Gender of the customer
  4. Education - Education of the customer in no. of years
  5. Marital Status - Marital status of the customer
  6. Usage - Avg. # times the customer wants to use the treadmill every week
  7. Fitness - Self rated fitness score of the customer (5 - very fit, 1 - very unfit)
  8. Income - Income of the customer
  9. Miles- Miles that a customer expects to run

# Function to create box and histogram plot 
def histogram_boxplot(data,feature,figsize=(15,7),kde=False,bins=None):
  f2, (ax_box2,ax_hist2) = plt.subplots(nrows=2,
                                        sharex=True,
                                        figsize=figsize,
                                        gridspec_kw={"height_ratios": (.25,.75)}
      )
  
  # Draw Box Plot
  sns.boxplot(data=data,x=feature,ax=ax_box2,showmeans=True,color="mediumturquoise")
  
  # Draw Histogram
  if bins:
    sns.histplot(data=data,x=feature,ax=ax_hist2,kde=kde,bins=bins,color="mediumpurple")
  else:
     sns.histplot(data=data,x=feature,ax=ax_hist2,kde=kde,color="mediumpurple")

  # Draw mean and median 
  ax_hist2.axvline(data[feature].mean(),color='green',linestyle='--')
  ax_hist2.axvline(data[feature].median(),color='black',linestyle='-')
